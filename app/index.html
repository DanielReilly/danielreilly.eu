About

I'm a junior web developer from Glasgow, Scotland.
I started working as a developer in February 2014 after a brief but useful time in Strathclyde University and in the period since, have had the privilege of meeting and offering my help to a wide range of developers, start-ups, and small businesses.

I enjoy going attending Hackathons, joining in and working on my development workflow. I am a huge text editor geek and have been known to get embarrassingly excited over new Sublime Text features or finding a cool new zsh command to add to my dotfiles.

The last few years have taken me as far afield as San Francisco where I visited a few Silicon Valley giants and attended Google I/O 2011 over three days.

Recently I have been gaining experience in the Angularjs, Flask and Web2py frameworks while helping build a web app. I continue to learn more and more about these technologies as I go along and hope to continue working with them for a number of years!

I enjoy using a wide range of methods and tools to develop and am always open to new ways of supercharging my workflow. If you have something new and exciting to show me, please contact me! Even if it's something you built. I'd love to give you my feedback on it. There's something very gratifying about being an early adopter.
